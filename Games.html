Terraforming Mars

Solo Project

Terraforming Mars is a complex board game by Jacob Fryxelius. For a university project, I created a digital version of the game in Unity, including the board and primary mechanics in a mostly intuitive way. I adapted the cards to engrain them in the system itself rather than keeping them as cards and added an AI enemy to play against.
