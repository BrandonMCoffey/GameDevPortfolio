Old Classroom

Autodesk Maya (Arnold)

An old classroom that was left years ago, affected by rust and other natural elements. All of the models in this environment are surfaced procedurally through Arnold. There are two textures used to drive the procedural texturing, one for scratches and one for the tiles in the floor. Everything was set up and rendered in Autodesk Mays with Arnold.
