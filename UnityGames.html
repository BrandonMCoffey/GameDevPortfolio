Tank Boss Battle

A boss battle against a massive tank! I'm a long fan of an old and simple game called Wii Play Tanks, and wanted to recreate some of those ideas into a tank minigame against a boss. On a more technical level, I implemented the observer pattern for more efficient game references, the state pattern for cleaner AI, and object pooling (for both sound effects and the bullets) for better performance.
