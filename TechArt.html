Technical Art

Wind Visualization

A Particle simulation that is based directly on data from a Wind Tunnel analyzing the wind coming off of a square-back car. The tool has a ton of customization options and runs in real-time using Unity, with additional support for VR using MRTK toolkit, as well as midair haptics using an ultrahaptics board (not shown).

No Way Home VFX Recreation

Houdini VFX

A recreation of a shot from Spiderman: No Way Home, involving models, composition, lighting, VFX, and rendering.

Prestige Lightning

Houdini and Nuke

From the movie, prestige, I recreated the Lightning effect and used Nuke to composite the shot together in layers, adding custom reflection and roto comps to create the final look of the shot.
