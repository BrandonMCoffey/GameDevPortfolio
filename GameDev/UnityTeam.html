Telemancer: Septembers Story

Programmer (Unity)

Telemancer: September's Story is part of the course, Game Lab, at UTD. Me and 40+ students spent Fall 2021 designing and developing the game, from the story to the art to the programming. I was in charge of Player Systems, which revolves around the the actions that a player can take and how they interact with the world. My work included the player's abilities, implementing animations, and setting up feedback and state systems.
