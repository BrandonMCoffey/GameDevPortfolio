Telemancer: Septembers Story

Programmer (Unity)

Telemancer: September's Story is part of a game development course at UTD where 40+ students design and develop a game to completion in one semester. I was in charge of programming Player Systems, which revolves around the the actions that a player can take and how they interact with the world. My work included the player's abilities, implementing animations, and setting up feedback and state systems.
